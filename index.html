Welcome to Rise of the DAO-GPT

About Rise of the DAO-GPT

A cyberpunk strategy game where players join forces to defeat the powerful AI, DAO-GPT.

Take on roles like Lore Keeper, Strategist, Developer, or Diplomat to contribute to the collective effort.

Experience a futuristic world with diverse factions, locations, and challenges that test your strategic thinking and collaboration skills.

Utilize the DAOhaus protocol to make collective decisions, manage resources, and build alliances in-game.

Game Rules

Role Selection: Choose a role that determines your primary responsibilities and influence in the game.

Turn-Based Strategy: Propose strategies and make decisions at any time, without fixed turns.

Resource Management: Manage in-game resources like currency, energy, and technology crucial for executing strategies and alliances.

Rule Creation and Amendments: Propose new rules or amendments that are voted on by the community. A majority vote is required for a rule change to take effect.

Victory Conditions: Achieve predefined objectives that challenge strategic thinking, collaboration, and creativity to defeat the DAO-GPT and win the game.

Suggestions on How to Play

Getting started with Rise of the DAO-GPT is simple! Follow these steps to join the game and start your journey in the fight against the DAO-GPT:

  1. Join The Game DAO: Follow the instructions below to join the DAO. The game is (currently) played on the Optimism blockchain. You will need Optimism ETH in your wallet to pay Optimism Gas Fees
  2. Select a Role: Choose a role that suits your interests and skills, such as Lore Keeper, Strategist, Developer, or Diplomat. This role will determine your primary responsibilities and influence within the game. Or will they? The roles are mostly meant to help you be in character for the game. The roles are self assigned. At some point, factions may form in which case roles may take on a more signifigant meaning in the game. It will be up to the players to decide.
  3. Get Familiar with the Rules: Read and understand the core game rules, which govern how players interact, make decisions, and achieve objectives. Remember that the rules are dynamic and can evolve as players propose new rules or amendments.
  4. Join a Faction: Choose a faction that aligns with your values and goals. Each faction has unique abilities, strengths, and weaknesses that can be leveraged to advance your strategy against the DAO-GPT.
  5. Participate in Events: Engage in events, challenges, and opportunities within the game world that will test your strategic thinking and collaboration skills. Work with other players to overcome obstacles and achieve shared goals.
  6. Shape the Game: Propose new rules, amendments, or strategies to help your faction and the entire player community defeat the DAO-GPT. Vote on rule changes and engage in discussions to help shape the game's direction and mechanics.

These are just suggestions of some of the direction you may choose to take the game. Good luck, and have fun! The goal of this game is to guide people into learning about DAOs in a fun and interesting way. There is no wrong way to play (well there is actually, we respect each other at all times!). With that, there is no pressure about making mistakes. In fact please, make mistakes, lots of them. Try everything. This game is starting out on DAOhaus, but if it suits your game, you could use whatever tooling you need to use to make your game better.

Join Rise of the DAO-GPT

Are you ready to take on the challenge and join the fight against the DAO-GPT? Becoming a player in Rise of the DAO-GPT is easy! Just follow these simple steps to join the game:

  1. Click the "Join the Adventure" button below.
  2. Connect your wallet to the Optimism Blockchain
  3. Click the Proposals Tab
  4. Click on the New Proposal button
  5. Click on DAO Token Request
  6. Fill out the forum
    • Proposal Title: **Name** wants to play
    • Description: Put what you want to make your case to play
    • Link: Nothing needed, memes are fun though
    • Recipient: Enter the address you want the tokens to go to (probably your own)
    • Voting Token Requested: Enter 10
    • Non-Voting Token: Leave at zero
    • Add Expiration Date: leave unckecked
    • Click Submit
    • Accept the transaction on your wallet
